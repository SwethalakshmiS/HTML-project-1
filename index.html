GOLDEN RETRIEVER


The Golden Retriever is a Scottish breed of retriever dog of medium size.
It is characterised by a gentle and affectionate nature and a striking golden coat.
It is commonly kept as a pet and is among the most frequently registered breeds in several Western countries.
Colors: Dark Golden, Light Golden, Cream, Golden
Temperament: Friendly, Intelligent, Kind, Reliable, Trustworthy, Confident
Life expectancy: 10 – 12 years
Origin: United Kingdom, Scotland, England
Weight: Female: 25–32 kg, Male: 30–34 kg, Height: Female: 51–56 cm, Male: 56–61 cm
The Kennel Club: standard
